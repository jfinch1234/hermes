# Lead Routing Rules

## Categories

### Not a fit (stop)
Criteria:
- Requests recommendations, rankings, or optimization.
- Asks for conversion or promotion outcomes.
- Rejects neutral narrowing or auditable constraints.

Who responds:
- Founder or designated intake owner.

Maximum touches:
- 1 response.

### Education only
Criteria:
- Curious or research-driven inquiries.
- No clear decision surface or product context.
- Wants public materials without a pilot path.

Who responds:
- Intake owner or content lead.

Maximum touches:
- 2 responses.

### Potential pilot
Criteria:
- Clear decision surface with meaningful variation.
- Acceptance of non-ranking, non-optimization behavior.
- Interest in auditable constraints and proof.

Who responds:
- Founder or pilot lead.

Maximum touches:
- 4 responses.

## General rules
- Do not move to a pilot without confirming non-ranking and non-optimization acceptance.
- Avoid urgency, pressure, or conversion framing.
- Keep responses short and factual.
