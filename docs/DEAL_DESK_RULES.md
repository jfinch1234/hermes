# Deal Desk Rules

## Pricing Floors (Non-Negotiable)
- Pilot: $2,500/mo per store
- Enterprise: $8,000/mo per store
- Platform License: $250,000 annual minimum

## Discount Policy
- Pilot: Max 10% discount, only for annual prepay
- Enterprise: Max 15% discount with multi-store commitment
- Platform: Negotiated, never below $250,000 annual

## Non-Negotiables
- No ranking
- No recommendations
- No paid placement
- No cross-store comparisons
- No policy overrides

## Walk-Away Triggers
- Requests for outcome guarantees
- Requests for placement or sponsorship
- Requests for ranking or ordering outputs
- Requests framed as conversion optimization

## Required Pilot Terms
- Usage logging enabled
- Regular feedback calls
- No public claims without prior approval
- Proof mode access for audit and verification
