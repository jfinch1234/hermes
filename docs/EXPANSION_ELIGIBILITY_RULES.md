# Expansion Eligibility Rules

Expansion is allowed only after a completed pilot and a positive closeout memo.

## Allowed expansion paths
- Additional stores (one at a time)
- Additional decision flows
- Additional users

## Explicitly forbidden
- Cross-store comparisons
- Ranking requests
- Paid placement

## Prerequisites for any expansion
- Signed enterprise agreement
- Updated scope definition
- Confirmed adherence to non-ranking and max-3
- Proof access remains non-production only

## Required re-validation steps
- Re-run TRUST_METRICS review
- Re-affirm PILOT_SUCCESS_SIGNALS
- Confirm no scope drift in the new flow
