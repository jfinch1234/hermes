# Pilot Observation Framework

## What is observed
- Language used by stakeholders when describing Hermes outputs
- Trust signals (comfort with neutrality, acceptance of non-ranking)
- Confusion points (misunderstood outputs, unclear differences)

## Who observes
- Founder
- Product lead
- Pilot operator

## What is explicitly not observed
- Conversion rates
- Outcomes or sales lift
- Optimization or persuasion effects

## How observations are recorded
- Short, structured notes
- No metrics or dashboards
- Notes tied to specific sessions or conversations
