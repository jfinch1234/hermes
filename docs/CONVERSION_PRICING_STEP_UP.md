# Conversion Pricing Step-Up

## Pilot to enterprise transition
- Pilots convert to enterprise pricing at the end of the pilot term.
- Pilots do not extend indefinitely.

## Minimum pricing floors
- Pilot: $2,500/mo per store
- Enterprise: $8,000/mo per store
- Platform license: $250,000 annual minimum

## Timing and commitment
- Conversion decision must be made at pilot end.
- Annual commitments are required for expansion.

## Pricing principles
- Pricing increases reflect trust and scope, not outcomes.

## Prohibited models
- Outcome-based pricing
- Success-fee models
